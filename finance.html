Financial Options

Visa image here, Master Card image here

At Castro Commons, we'll submit your insurance claim forms electronically for your convenience. To facilitate the insurance process, it will be helpful to have your insurance information prior to your first appointment. Every attempt is made to contact your insurance to get the best estimate of your coverage. Please note that it is only an estimate from your insurance carrier. All payments are due the day services are rendered unless other financial arrangements are made with the office manager.

We accept cash, check, money order, Visa, and Master Card.

We understand that unexpected dental bills can sometimes strain finances.If this happens, talk with us immediately by calling us at 650.965.1595. If you help us keep the lines of communication open, we can usually work out acceptable arrangements.

Still have questions?

Please do drop by at our office in Mountain View and speak to our friendly staff.
