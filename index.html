Our Commitment

Our dental practice helps patients understand their present dental conditions, diagnosis, and treatment options so that they can make informed decisions regarding their individual needs.

We believe each of us have a range of dental health goals. It is important for patients to find a match between their health philosophies and ours. Our dental practice helps patients understand their present dental conditions, diagnosis, and treatment options so that they can make informed decisions regarding their individual needs. Only one patient is scheduled at a time and no one is rushed in or out. Sound science and proven research is used to develop long-term solutions to your dental needs. More importantly, we value mutually rewarding and lasting relationships with our patients. We will work together as a team to reach your dental health goals.

Our compassionate and understanding staff takes pride in paying close attention to each patient's concerns. Should a dental emergency occur, we make every effort to see and care for you as soon as possible. Infection control is extremely important to us. We strictly maintain sterilization protocols recommended by the American Dental Association (ADA), the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), and the Center for Disease Control (CDC). We use only latex-free products and distilled water in the dental water lines. We want you to be confident knowing that we hold ourselves to the highest standards.

We understand how uneasy some may feel about their dental visits. We strive to provide a positive and personable environment for our patients. Thank you for taking the time visiting our website. We look forward to serving you.
